## General Content Strategy

### Post Types (Rotate Weekly):

1. **Park Features** (2x/week)
   - Highlight specific parks
   - Share unique amenities
   - Feature park reviews

2. **Dog Care Tips** (2x/week)
   - Indoor vs outdoor park benefits
   - Socialization tips
   - Health & safety advice
   - Training tips

3. **Blog Promotion** (2x/week)
   - Link to new blog posts
   - Quote key insights
   - Ask engaging questions

4. **Community Engagement** (1x/week)
   - Ask questions
   - Share user stories
   - Run polls
   - Respond to comments

### Best Practices:

✅ **Post Frequency**: 3-5 posts per week (start with daily for first week)
✅ **Posting Times**: 8-9 AM PT, 12-1 PM PT, 5-6 PM PT
✅ **Hashtags**: Use 2-3 relevant hashtags per post
✅ **Engage**: Respond to all comments within 24 hours
✅ **Visuals**: Include images when possible (park photos, infographics)
✅ **Links**: Always include link to website when relevant
✅ **Value First**: Provide tips and value before promoting
