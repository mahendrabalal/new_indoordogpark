# Instagram Setup Guide - Step-by-Step

## 🤔 FAQ: Do I Need a Separate Account?

### Instagram doesn't have "Pages" like Facebook - only accounts!

**Answer:** You need a **separate business account**, but you can use the same email/login and switch between accounts easily on your phone.

---

## ✅ Option 1: Create Separate Business Account (RECOMMENDED)

### Why Create a Separate Account?
- ✅ Professional - business content stays separate from personal
- ✅ Branding - business-focused bio and content
- ✅ Analytics - business insights and metrics
- ✅ Management - can assign someone else later
- ✅ Easy switching - access both accounts from same phone

### Step-by-Step Setup:

#### **On Mobile App (Easiest):**

1. **Open Instagram App** on your phone
2. **Add New Account:**
   - Tap your profile picture (bottom right)
   - Tap your username at the top
   - Tap "Add account" or "+" icon
   - OR: Go to Settings → Scroll down → "Add account"

3. **Create New Account:**
   - Tap "Create new account"
   - Enter email (you can use a different email or same one)
   - OR: Use phone number (different from personal)
   - Choose username: `@indoordogpark` (check availability)
   - Create password
   - Complete account creation

4. **Convert to Business Account:**
   - Go to Settings (gear icon)
   - Tap "Account"
   - Tap "Switch to Professional Account"
   - Choose "Business" (not Creator)
   - Connect to Facebook page (if you have one)
   - Add business contact info

5. **Set Up Profile:**
   - Add profile picture (logo - 400x400px square)
   - Add business name: "Indoor Dog Park - California"
   - Add bio (see template below)
   - Add website: `https://www.indoordogpark.org`

6. **Switch Between Accounts:**
   - Tap and hold your profile picture (bottom right)
   - See all your accounts listed
   - Tap the account you want to use
   - Instagram remembers which account you're using

#### **On Desktop (instagram.com):**

1. Go to instagram.com and log in
2. Click your profile picture (top right) → Settings
3. Scroll down → "Switch Accounts" → "Add Account"
4. Create new account with business email/username
5. Convert to Business account (same steps as mobile)

---

## ⚠️ Option 2: Convert Personal Account (NOT RECOMMENDED)

**Only do this if:**
- You don't mind mixing personal and business content
- You want everything in one place
- You don't plan to have someone else manage it

**Steps:**
1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Tap "Switch to Professional Account"
3. Choose "Business"
4. Connect to Facebook page
5. Add business info

**Downsides:**
- Personal and business content mixed together
- Less professional appearance
- Harder to separate later

## 🔄 Managing Multiple Accounts

### Switching Between Accounts:

**On Mobile:**
- Tap and hold your profile picture (bottom right)
- See all accounts listed
- Tap to switch

**On Desktop:**
- Click your profile picture (top right)
- Click your username → "Switch Accounts"
- Select account

### Best Practices:
- ✅ Always check which account you're using before posting
- ✅ Use different profile pictures to distinguish accounts
- ✅ Set up business account notifications separately

   - Better for analytics

   - Helps with cross-posting
   - Better analytics integration

## 🚨 Common Questions

**Q: Can I use the same email for both accounts?**
A: Yes! Instagram allows multiple accounts with the same email.

**Q: Can I switch accounts easily?**
A: Yes! Just tap and hold your profile picture to see all accounts.

**Q: Will my personal followers see my business account?**
A: Only if you follow them from your business account, or if they search for it.

**Q: Can I have someone else manage my business account?**
A: Yes! You can:
- Give them login credentials (not ideal)
- Use Instagram's "Account Access" feature (better)
- Use Meta Business Suite to assign roles (best)

**Q: What if the username is taken?**
A: Try:
- `@indoordogparkorg`
- `@indoordogparkca`
- `@indoordogparkcalifornia`
- `@indoordogparkdirectory`
