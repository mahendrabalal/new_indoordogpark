# Instagram Setup Guide for IndoorDogPark.org

This guide will help you create and optimize your Instagram business account for your indoor dog park directory website.

## Step 1: Create Your Instagram Business Account

### ⚠️ Important: Should You Use a Separate Account?

**YES - Create a separate business account for IndoorDogPark.org** (Recommended)

#### Why Create a Separate Business Account?

✅ **Professional Brand Image**
- Clean, focused content specifically for indoor dog parks
- No mixing of personal photos with business content
- Looks more professional to park owners, visitors, and partners

✅ **Better Business Features**
- Full access to Instagram Business Insights (analytics)
- Instagram Shopping features (if you add them later)
- Ability to run ads more effectively
- Contact buttons (email, phone)
- Links in Stories (requires 10K followers, but business accounts get priority)

✅ **Content Strategy**
- Your personal account likely has varied content
- Business account should focus 100% on dog parks
- Followers want consistent, relevant content

✅ **Privacy & Separation**
- Keep your personal life separate from business
- Can manage business account professionally
- Easier to have someone else help manage it later

✅ **Account Security**
- If personal account gets compromised, business stays safe
- Different passwords = better security
- Can use business email for recovery

✅ **Analytics & Growth**
- Business accounts get detailed analytics
- Track follower demographics (helpful for targeting)
- See what content works best for your business audience
- Better understanding of your dog park community

❌ **Potential Downsides of Using Personal Account:**
- Mixed content confuses your audience
- Less professional appearance
- Harder to track business-specific metrics
- Personal posts might not align with business goals
- Difficult to hand off management to team members later

### Option A: Create a New Business Account (Recommended)

1. **Download Instagram App** (Mobile) or go to [instagram.com](https://instagram.com) (Desktop)
2. **Sign Up**:
   - Use your business email (e.g., hello@indoordogpark.org) - **Use a different email than your personal account**
   - Choose a username: `@indoordogpark` or `@indoordogparkorg`
   - Create a strong password (different from personal account)
   - Complete your profile information

3. **Convert to Business Account** (if not already business):

   **On Desktop (Instagram.com):**
   - Click your profile picture → **Edit profile**
   - Or go to **Settings** (⚙️ icon) → **Settings and privacy** → **Account type and tools**
   - Click **Switch to Professional Account** (or **Switch account type** if already professional)
   - Select **Business** (not Creator - Creator is for influencers/artists)
   - Choose category: **Pets** or **Local Business** or **Community Organization**
   - Connect to a Facebook Page (you can create one during this process)

   **On Mobile App:**
   - Go to your profile (tap your profile picture bottom right)
   - Tap the three lines (☰) top right → **Settings and privacy**
   - Tap **Account type and tools**
   - Tap **Switch to Professional Account** (or **Switch account type**)
   - Select **Business**
   - Choose category and connect Facebook Page

   **Note:** If you see "Hashtags Metrics" in your sidebar, you might already be on a Professional account! Check if it says "Business" or "Creator" in settings.

### Option B: Convert Existing Personal Account (Not Recommended)

**Only do this if:**
- Your personal account has very few followers
- You rarely use your personal account
- You don't mind mixing business and personal content

**If you do convert personal account:**
1. Open Instagram app → **Settings** → **Account**
2. Tap **Switch to Professional Account**
3. Select **Business**
4. Connect to a Facebook Page (create one if needed)
5. Clean up old personal posts that don't fit your brand

## Step 3: Set Up Instagram Business Features

### Connect Facebook Page
1. Go to **Settings** → **Business**
2. Connect or create a Facebook Page for IndoorDogPark
3. This enables advanced features and analytics

### Enable Business Features
- **Instagram Insights**: Track follower growth, engagement, and best posting times
- **Instagram Shopping**: Not applicable unless you sell products
- **Promoted Posts/Ads**: Available once connected to Facebook

### Add Action Buttons
- **Email**: Add contact email
- **Call**: Only if you have a phone number
- **Directions**: Not needed for directory website
- **Text**: Not needed

## Step 4: Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (What to Post)

#### 1. **Featured Parks** (40% of content)
- High-quality photos of indoor dog parks from your directory
- Include park name, location, and key features
- Use hashtags like: #indoordogpark #dogpark #dogplaytime
- Tag the park if they have Instagram (with permission)

**Post Ideas:**
- "This week's featured park: [Park Name] in [City]"
- "Best indoor parks for small dogs"
- "Climate-controlled play spaces for hot/cold days"

#### 2. **Educational Content** (25% of content)
- Tips for dog owners
- Benefits of indoor dog parks
- How to prepare for a visit
- Safety tips

**Post Ideas:**
- "5 Benefits of Indoor Dog Parks"
- "What to Bring to an Indoor Dog Park"
- "Indoor vs Outdoor: Which is Right for Your Dog?"

#### 3. **User-Generated Content** (20% of content)
- Repost photos from park visitors (with permission and credit)
- Create a branded hashtag: #IndoorDogParkLife or #IndoorDogParkDirectory
- Encourage followers to tag you: @indoordogpark

**Post Ideas:**
- "Thanks to @username for sharing this amazing park!"
- "Tag us in your indoor dog park adventures!"

#### 4. **New Listings & Updates** (10% of content)
- Announce new parks added to directory
- Website updates and features
- Blog post highlights

#### 5. **Behind the Scenes** (5% of content)
- Team introductions (if applicable)
- How you research and verify parks
- Website development updates

### Content Formats

#### Regular Posts
- **Photos**: Square (1080x1080px) or Portrait (1080x1350px)
- **Carousels**: Multiple images telling a story
- **Videos**: 15-60 seconds, engaging and informative

#### Stories (Post daily)
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Quick updates, polls, Q&As
- **Park Spotlights**: Quick features of individual parks
- **Tips**: Quick tips in text or video format
- **Highlights**: Save best stories to profile (e.g., "Featured Parks", "Tips", "FAQ")

#### Reels (Post 2-3x per week)
- **Quick tours**: Virtual tours of parks (with permission)
- **Tips in 30 seconds**: Quick educational content
- **Trending sounds**: Use popular sounds with dog park-related content
- **Before/After**: Transform a space or show park features

## Step 6: Posting Schedule

### Recommended Frequency
- **Posts**: 3-5 times per week
- **Stories**: Daily (at least 1-2 stories)
- **Reels**: 2-3 times per week

### Best Times to Post (Test and adjust)
- **Mornings**: 7-9 AM (weekdays)
- **Lunch**: 11 AM - 1 PM (weekdays)
- **Evenings**: 5-7 PM (weekdays)
- **Weekends**: 8-10 AM, 2-4 PM

**Monday**: Featured Park

**Wednesday**: New Listing or Update
**Thursday**: User-Generated Content
**Friday**: Fun/Engaging Post or Reel

## Step 9: Analytics & Optimization

### Track Key Metrics
- **Followers**: Growth rate (aim for 5-10% monthly)
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes + Comments / Followers (aim for 2-5%)
- **Reach**: How many unique accounts see your content
- **Impressions**: Total number of times content is viewed
- **Website Clicks**: From bio link
- **Profile Visits**: How many people check your profile

### Review Weekly
- Which posts performed best?
- What time did your audience engage most?
- Which hashtags generated most reach?
- What content type (photo/reel/carousel) works best?

### Adjust Strategy
- Double down on what works
- Stop or modify what doesn't
- Test new content formats
- Experiment with posting times

## Step 10: Add Instagram Link to Your Website

Once your Instagram account is created:

1. **Get your Instagram URL**:
   - Go to your Instagram profile
   - Copy the URL (e.g., `https://www.instagram.com/indoordogpark/`)

2. **Add to Environment Variables**:
   - Open your `.env.local` file (or create if it doesn't exist)
   - Add: `NEXT_PUBLIC_SOCIAL_INSTAGRAM=https://www.instagram.com/yourusername/`
   - Save the file

3. **Restart Your Development Server**:
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```

4. **Verify**:
   - Check your website footer
   - Instagram icon should appear and link to your account
   - Test the link to ensure it works

### Alternative: Add to Meta Tags

You can also add Instagram to your site's metadata in `src/app/layout.tsx`:
```typescript
social: {
  instagram: 'https://www.instagram.com/yourusername/',
}
```

## Pro Tips

1. **Consistency is Key**: Post regularly and maintain a consistent aesthetic
2. **Quality Over Quantity**: Better to post 3 great posts than 7 mediocre ones
3. **Be Authentic**: Share real experiences and genuine recommendations
4. **Engage Authentically**: Build relationships, don't just push your website
5. **Use User Content**: People trust peer recommendations more than brand posts
6. **Monitor Competitors**: Learn from similar accounts, but stay unique
7. **Track What Works**: Use insights to guide your content strategy
8. **Be Patient**: Growth takes time, especially organically
9. **Cross-Platform**: Share Instagram content on your website/blog
10. **Stay Updated**: Instagram features change frequently, stay current

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Buying followers (hurts engagement rate and reach)
❌ Using irrelevant hashtags (can get shadowbanned)
❌ Ignoring comments and DMs (hurts engagement)
❌ Posting inconsistently (algorithm favors active accounts)
❌ Only posting promotional content (people will unfollow)
❌ Not using Instagram Stories (miss out on engagement)
❌ Overlooking Reels (highest reach potential)
❌ Ignoring analytics (you won't know what works)
