# Content Update Strategy for AdSense Compliance

This document outlines the strategy for regularly updating content on IndoorDogPark.org to maintain AdSense compliance and provide value to users.

## Content Update Requirements

Google AdSense requires that sites have **regularly updated, unique content** to maintain approval. This strategy ensures we meet those requirements.

## Update Frequency

### Daily Updates
- **Park Data**: Automated hourly refresh of park information (via `revalidate: 3600`)
- **User Reviews**: New reviews added as users submit them
- **Park Submissions**: New listings processed and published

### Weekly Updates
- **Statistics**: Update park counts, review totals, and city statistics
- **Featured Parks**: Rotate featured listings
- **Search Analytics**: Review popular searches and optimize content

### Monthly Updates
- **Blog Posts**: Publish 2-4 new blog articles
  - Topics: Dog park guides, seasonal tips, city spotlights, training advice
  - Minimum 800 words per article
  - Original content with expert insights
- **Guides**: Update or add new guide content
- **FAQ**: Add new questions based on user inquiries
- **City Pages**: Refresh statistics and add new parks

### Quarterly Updates
- **Major Content Refresh**: Review and update all static pages
- **About Page**: Update team information, statistics, milestones
- **How It Works**: Update with new features and improvements
- **Resource Pages**: Add new downloadable resources

## Content Types to Update

### 1. Park Listings
**Update Frequency**: Continuous
- New park submissions
- Updated park information (hours, pricing, amenities)
- New photos from park owners
- User reviews and ratings
- Status updates (open/closed, seasonal changes)

**Automation**: 
- API integration for real-time updates
- User submission forms
- Admin review process

### 2. Blog Content
**Update Frequency**: 2-4 posts per month
**Content Ideas**:
- "Best Indoor Dog Parks in [City]"
- "How to Prepare Your Dog for Indoor Play"
- "Seasonal Dog Park Tips"
- "Training Your Dog for Social Play"
- "What to Bring to an Indoor Dog Park"
- "Understanding Dog Park Etiquette"
- "Indoor vs Outdoor Dog Parks: Pros and Cons"
- "Finding Dog-Friendly Establishments Near You"

**Requirements**:
- Minimum 800 words
- Original content (not scraped or copied)
- Expert insights or personal experiences
- SEO-optimized with relevant keywords
- Include images and proper formatting

### 3. City Pages
**Update Frequency**: Monthly
- Add new parks discovered in each city
- Update statistics (park count, average ratings)
- Refresh featured parks
- Update local insights and recommendations
- Add seasonal information

### 4. Guides and Resources
**Update Frequency**: Monthly
- Add new guide topics based on user questions
- Update existing guides with new information
- Create downloadable resources (checklists, worksheets)
- Add video content (when available)

### 5. FAQ Section
**Update Frequency**: As needed (typically monthly)
- Add new questions from user inquiries
- Update answers with current information
- Organize by category for better navigation
- Link to relevant blog posts or guides

## Content Quality Standards

### Originality
- ✅ All content must be original or significantly transformed
- ✅ No direct copying from other sources
- ✅ Add unique insights, experiences, or analysis
- ✅ Proper attribution when referencing external sources

### Value
- ✅ Content must provide substantial value to users
- ✅ Answer real questions and solve problems
- ✅ Include actionable advice and tips
- ✅ Use clear, readable language

### Length
- **Blog Posts**: Minimum 800 words, target 1,200-2,000 words
- **Park Descriptions**: 100-300 words per park
- **City Pages**: 200-500 words of unique content
- **Guides**: 500-1,500 words depending on topic
- **FAQ Answers**: 50-200 words per answer

### SEO Optimization
- Use relevant keywords naturally
- Include meta descriptions
- Proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3)
- Internal linking to related content
- Image alt text and captions

## Compliance Checklist

Before publishing any content, verify:
- [ ] Content is original or significantly transformed
- [ ] Minimum word count met
- [ ] Value provided to users
- [ ] SEO optimized
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Links are functional
- [ ] Mobile-responsive
- [ ] No copyright violations
- [ ] Fact-checked
- [ ] Proofread

## Emergency Updates

If AdSense flags content issues:
1. Review flagged content immediately
2. Remove or fix problematic content
3. Update content to meet requirements
4. Resubmit for review if needed
5. Document lessons learned

## Success Metrics

**Target Goals**:
- 2-4 blog posts per month
- 10+ new park listings per month
- 50+ new reviews per month
- 1-2 new guides per month
- 5+ FAQ updates per month

**Quality Indicators**:
- Average time on page > 2 minutes
- Bounce rate < 60%
- Pages per session > 2
- Returning visitor rate > 30%
