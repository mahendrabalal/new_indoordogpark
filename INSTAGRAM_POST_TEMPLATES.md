🛡️ Dog Park Safety Tips

## Tips for Using These Templates

1. **Customize with Real Data**: When featuring parks, use actual park names, addresses, and ratings from your directory
2. **Add Your Own Photos**: Use photos from your park database or create simple graphics
3. **Engage with Comments**: Always respond to comments to build community
4. **Update Bio Link**: Change your bio link occasionally to promote specific pages (new blogs, featured parks, etc.)
5. **Post Consistently**: 3-5 posts per week works well
6. **Use Stories Daily**: Post quick tips, behind-the-scenes, Q&As in Stories
