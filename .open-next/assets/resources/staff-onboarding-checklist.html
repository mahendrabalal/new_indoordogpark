✨ How to Use This Checklist

Print this checklist and use it for each new staff member. Check off items as you complete them. This ensures nothing is missed and helps new employees feel prepared.

📋 Pre-First Day (Before They Start)

☐ Paperwork & Documentation

  • ☐ Employment contract signed
  • ☐ W-4 tax form completed
  • ☐ I-9 verification completed
  • ☐ Direct deposit form (if applicable)
  • ☐ Emergency contact information collected
  • ☐ Background check completed
  • ☐ Reference checks completed

☐ Preparation

  • ☐ Schedule first day orientation
  • ☐ Prepare workspace/station
  • ☐ Order uniform/name tag (if applicable)
  • ☐ Set up email account (if applicable)
  • ☐ Prepare welcome packet
  • ☐ Notify existing staff of new hire

🎯 First Day Orientation

☐ Welcome & Introduction

  • ☐ Welcome meeting with owner/manager
  • ☐ Tour of facility
  • ☐ Introduction to team members
  • ☐ Review facility layout and key areas
  • ☐ Explain parking and break areas

☐ Policies & Procedures

  • ☐ Review employee handbook
  • ☐ Explain work schedule and hours
  • ☐ Review time-off policy
  • ☐ Explain dress code/uniform requirements
  • ☐ Review safety protocols
  • ☐ Explain emergency procedures
  • ☐ Review break and meal policies

☐ Role Expectations

  • ☐ Review job description
  • ☐ Explain daily responsibilities
  • ☐ Set performance expectations
  • ☐ Review key performance indicators (if applicable)
  • ☐ Explain reporting structure
  • ☐ Discuss growth opportunities

🐕 Dog Park Specific Training

☐ Dog Safety & Handling

  • ☐ Dog body language basics
  • ☐ Safe dog handling techniques
  • ☐ Recognizing signs of aggression or stress
  • ☐ How to break up dog fights (if applicable)
  • ☐ Understanding different dog sizes and temperaments
  • ☐ Vaccination requirements and verification

☐ Facility Operations

  • ☐ How to check in/out dogs
  • ☐ Cleaning procedures and schedule
  • ☐ Equipment operation (gates, doors, etc.)
  • ☐ Maintenance reporting procedures
  • ☐ Supply inventory and ordering
  • ☐ Opening and closing procedures

☐ Customer Service Standards

  • ☐ Greeting customers warmly
  • ☐ Answering common questions
  • ☐ Handling complaints professionally
  • ☐ Membership sales process (if applicable)
  • ☐ Phone etiquette
  • ☐ Email communication standards
  • ☐ Social media guidelines (if applicable)

📚 Training Videos & Resources

☐ Watch Training Videos

  • ☐ Emergency response procedures
  • ☐ Dog fight prevention and intervention
  • ☐ Cleaning and sanitization protocols
  • ☐ Customer service best practices
  • ☐ Point-of-sale system training (if applicable)
  • ☐ Safety equipment usage

Note: Create or link to your training videos. Update this list with your specific resources.

☐ Review Documentation

  • ☐ Emergency response plan
  • ☐ Incident reporting procedures
  • ☐ Vaccination verification process
  • ☐ Waiver and liability forms
  • ☐ Facility rules and regulations
  • ☐ Equipment manuals (if applicable)

👨‍🏫 Shadowing & Hands-On Training

☐ Week 1: Observation

  • ☐ Shadow experienced staff member (Day 1-2)
  • ☐ Observe customer interactions
  • ☐ Watch dog handling procedures
  • ☐ Observe cleaning routines
  • ☐ Ask questions and take notes

☐ Week 1-2: Guided Practice

  • ☐ Practice customer check-ins with supervision
  • ☐ Practice dog handling with supervision
  • ☐ Practice cleaning procedures
  • ☐ Handle phone calls with support
  • ☐ Process transactions with guidance

☐ Week 2-3: Independent Work

  • ☐ Work independently with backup available
  • ☐ Handle routine tasks without supervision
  • ☐ Escalate issues appropriately
  • ☐ Complete end-of-shift duties

✅ Completion & Follow-Up

☐ First Week Review

  • ☐ Schedule check-in meeting (end of week 1)
  • ☐ Review what's going well
  • ☐ Address any questions or concerns
  • ☐ Provide additional training if needed
  • ☐ Set goals for week 2

☐ 30-Day Review

  • ☐ Schedule 30-day performance review
  • ☐ Assess progress and competency
  • ☐ Gather feedback from employee
  • ☐ Discuss long-term goals
  • ☐ Address any ongoing training needs

☐ Final Sign-Off

  • ☐ All training completed
  • ☐ Employee feels confident in role
  • ☐ Manager/owner approval
  • ☐ Employee signature: _________________ Date: _______
  • ☐ Manager signature: _________________ Date: _______
