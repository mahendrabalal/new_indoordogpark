- **Opportunity:** Low competition with decent search volume = high ranking potential

### 2. Content Structure

#### Introduction Section (300-400 words)
- Hook: Address Long Island's weather challenges (cold winters, hot summers)
- Pain points: Limited outdoor options during extreme weather
- Solution: Indoor dog parks as year-round solution
- Include keyword in first paragraph naturally

#### Location-Specific Sections

**Nassau County Focus:**
- Towns: Garden City, Hempstead, Mineola, Rockville Centre, Valley Stream, etc.
- Highlight facilities in these areas
- Use "indoor dog park nassau county" variations

**Suffolk County Focus:**
- Towns: Huntington, Babylon, Islip, Brookhaven, Riverhead, etc.
- Highlight facilities in these areas
- Use "indoor dog park suffolk county" variations

#### Individual Facility Listings (8-12 parks)
For each facility include:
- Name and exact location
- Address with city/town
- Website link
- Phone number
- Key features (indoor play space, daycare, boarding, etc.)
- Pricing information (if available)
- What makes it unique
- "Best for" section
- Reviews/ratings data

#### Data Visualization Sections
- Map showing locations across Long Island
- Comparison charts (pricing, amenities, services)
- Distribution by county/town
- Features comparison table

#### FAQ Section (10-15 questions)
Target voice search queries:
- "Are there indoor dog parks on Long Island?"
- "What is the best indoor dog park in Long Island?"
- "How much does an indoor dog park cost on Long Island?"
- "Do indoor dog parks require vaccinations?"
- "Are indoor dog parks safe for small dogs?"

#### Tips and Best Practices Section
- How to choose the right indoor dog park
- What to bring
- Vaccination requirements
- Behavioral expectations
- Cost considerations
