✨ How It Works (Automatic!)

What Happens Automatically:

  • ✅ Expiration dates calculate automatically - Just enter the vaccination date
  • ✅ Days until expiry updates daily - Shows how many days are left
  • ✅ Status updates automatically - Shows "Current", "Expiring Soon" (within 30 days), or "EXPIRED"
  • ✅ Overall status combines everything - See at a glance which dogs need attention

You just need to: Enter vaccination dates when dogs visit. Everything else calculates itself!

📧 Sending Reminder Emails (Simple Methods)

✅ Easy Method: Filter & Send (Recommended for Most Owners)

  1. Open your tracker in Google Sheets or Excel
  2. Filter by "Overall Status" - Click the filter icon, select "Expiring Soon" or "EXPIRED"
  3. Copy the email addresses from the filtered list
  4. Send a group email using your regular email (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  5. Update "Last Reminder Sent" column with today's date

💡 Tip: Do this once a week or month - takes just 5 minutes!

🤖 Advanced: Automated Email Reminders (Optional - For Tech-Savvy Owners)

This sets up automatic emails that send themselves. Only do this if you're comfortable with technology.

Google Sheets Automated Emails:

  1. Open your tracker in Google Sheets
  2. Go to Extensions → Apps Script
  3. Paste the script code (available in full template)
  4. Set up a daily/weekly trigger

Note: This requires some technical setup. If you're not comfortable, stick with the simple filter method above!
