## 📱 Account Setup - Quick Checklist

### Instagram Setup (NEXT PRIORITY)

**⚠️ Important: Instagram doesn't have "Pages" like Facebook - only accounts!**
- You'll create a **separate business account** (different from your personal)
- You can use the same email/login and switch between accounts easily
- Instagram allows up to 5 accounts per login

**Steps:**
1. **Option A: Create New Account (Recommended)**
   - Open Instagram app (on your phone) or go to instagram.com
   - Tap your profile picture (bottom right) → Your username (top) → "Add account"
   - OR: Settings → Add account → Create new account
   - Enter business email (or use different phone number)
   - Choose username: `@indoordogpark` (or `@indoordogparkorg` if taken)

2. **Option B: Convert Personal Account (Not Recommended)**
   - Only do this if you don't mind mixing personal and business content
   - Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account → Business

3. **Set Up Business Account:**
   - Go to Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account
   - Choose "Business" (not Creator)
   - Connect to Facebook page (create page first if needed)
   - Add contact info (website, email)

4. **Switch Between Accounts:**
   - Tap and hold your profile picture (bottom right) to see all accounts
   - Tap the account you want to use
   - Instagram remembers which account you're using

**Why Separate Account is Better:**
- ✅ Professional appearance (no personal content mixing)
- ✅ Business branding separate from personal
- ✅ Can assign someone else to manage it later
- ✅ Business analytics and insights
- ✅ Can still access both from same phone/app

## 💡 Quick Tips

1. **Start Small**: Master 2-3 platforms before adding more
2. **Be Consistent**: Post at same times daily
3. **Engage Daily**: Respond to comments within 24 hours
4. **Use Tools**: Schedule posts with Later, Buffer, or Meta Business Suite
5. **Repurpose Content**: One video/post can work on multiple platforms
6. **Track Analytics**: Check what works and double down

---

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Creating accounts but not posting regularly
2. ❌ Ignoring comments and engagement
3. ❌ Posting same content everywhere (adapt for each platform)
4. ❌ Using too many hashtags (10-15 is ideal)
5. ❌ Not having a consistent brand/personality
6. ❌ Forgetting to link back to website
