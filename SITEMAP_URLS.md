## 🔍 Where to Submit

### Google Search Console
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Select your property: `https://www.indoordogpark.org`
3. Navigate to: **Sitemaps** (left sidebar)
4. Submit: `https://www.indoordogpark.org/sitemap.xml`
5. Click: **Submit**

**Note:** Google will automatically discover all sub-sitemaps from the index.

### Bing Webmaster Tools
1. Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Select your site: `https://www.indoordogpark.org`
3. Navigate to: **Sitemaps** (left sidebar)
4. Submit: `https://www.indoordogpark.org/sitemap.xml`
5. Click: **Submit**
